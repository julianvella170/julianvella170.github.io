PUPPYPRO

PuppyPro is a school project were I had to create a product that is paired with an
application. It works like a smart watch for humans however it is a dog collar which is
able to track your dogs steps, heartrate, sleep recod, calories burned, body temperature
and blood oxegen leven of your pet.

The dog collar is also a tracker, which keep track of where your pet is located at all
times just in case it gets lost.

The first step of the project was the case study where I had to understand how a tracker
works and how it gathers data, and compared a few smart dog collar apps and how
they work with their products, providing screenshots of their applications.

After that, I wrote down what features do I want my application to have including how
will my prototype look like and what features do i want my product to look like.

Then, it was time to start designing the prototype. I first started to design the low fidalety
wireframe to get a general idea of how I wanted my app to look like without making any
final decisions, but my idea of the app was quitle clear of how I wanted it to look like.

After the low fidalety, I created the final prototype along with the colours and fonts that I
wanted to use.
