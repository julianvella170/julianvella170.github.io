FUNDERPRO

FunderPro is a stock website where users are able to trade their stocks. FunderPro lets
their users invest a lot more money than regular trading sites.

I had to create a prototype for a company called RedAcre which owns FunderPro which
eventually helped me land an internship at RedAcre, and told me to redesign the
FunderPro website, and try to find ways to improve it.

The only specific guidelines given where the colours scheme I had to use and to use their
official sponsor, F2 star Ollie Bearman.

I wanted to create the prototype using only cirlces and shapes and in 2 days I managed
to create a home page, a contact page and an FAQ page. This was then submitted to
the chairperson on RedAcre
